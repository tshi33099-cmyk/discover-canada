Major Cities

Discover the urban heartbeats of the nation.

Toronto, Ontario

Canada's largest city, a global financial hub known for the CN Tower, diverse neighborhoods, and vibrant arts scene.

Vancouver, BC

A coastal seaport city surrounded by mountains. Famous for its film industry, outdoor lifestyle, and mild climate.

Montreal, Quebec

The cultural capital, known for its European flair, bilingual heritage, festivals like Jazz Fest, and historic Old Port.

Ottawa, Ontario

The nation's capital. Home to Parliament Hill, national museums, and the Rideau Canal, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Calgary, Alberta

Famous for the Calgary Stampede, this city is the gateway to the Rockies and a hub for Canada's energy industry.
