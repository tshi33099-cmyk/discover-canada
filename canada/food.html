Food & Cuisine

From sweet maple treats to savory classics.

Poutine

The ultimate Canadian comfort food. French fries topped with cheese curds and smothered in hot gravy. Originating from Quebec.

Maple Syrup

Canada produces 71% of the world's pure maple syrup. It's liquid gold, perfect on pancakes or used in cooking.

Nanaimo Bars

A no-bake dessert bar from Nanaimo, BC. It consists of a wafer crumb base, custard-flavoured butter icing, and melted chocolate.

Atlantic Seafood

The East Coast is famous for its fresh lobster, scallops, and chowder. A must-try for any seafood lover.

Montreal Bagels

Distinct from New York bagels, these are smaller, denser, sweeter, and boiled in honey-sweetened water before baking in a wood-fired oven.

The Caesar

Canada's national cocktail. Made with vodka, clamato juice, hot sauce, and Worcestershire sauce, usually garnished with celery.
