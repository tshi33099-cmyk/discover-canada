Travel Tips

Everything you need to know before you go.

Best Time to Visit

Summer (Jun-Aug) is warm and popular. Fall (Sep-Oct) offers beautiful foliage. Winter (Dec-Mar) is perfect for snow sports.

Entry Requirements

Most visitors need an eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization) or a visa. Check the official government website before booking.

Currency

The currency is the Canadian Dollar (CAD). Credit cards are widely accepted, but it's good to have some cash for small purchases.

Getting Around

Canada is huge! For long distances, flying is best. VIA Rail offers scenic train routes. Renting a car is great for exploring nature.

Nature Safety

When hiking, be aware of wildlife like bears. Keep a safe distance and never feed animals. Check weather conditions.

Tipping Etiquette

Tipping is customary in Canada. 15-20% is standard for restaurant service, taxis, and personal services.
